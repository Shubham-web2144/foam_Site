About

Good things happen when everything connects

Foam Pak US has been manufacturing foam and plastic food take out containers in Texas, U.S.A since 2007. Improving the customer satisfaction is our ultimate goal. we are striving to provide the best quality and competitive price of packaging products for the food industry. Over the years, We have garnered the trust and loyalty of countless customers by providing them with high quality food packaging products made with the latest technologies. Minimizing environmental pollution is our top responsibility. we always care about utilizing environmentally friendly manufacturing methods to avoid industrial pollution and strengthening environmental protections.
